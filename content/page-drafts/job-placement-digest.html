Production debugging: the focus ring disappeared after deployment

Symptom

Keyboard focus rings appeared locally but disappeared after deployment.

Root cause

Parent global styles referenced a removed palette token; production optimization reordered the stylesheets, allowing the invalid declaration to win.

Fix

I increased selector specificity without discarding the parent’s other button rules.

Prevention

I updated the Chrome verifier to reproduce production stylesheet order and assert the focus ring across viewports. The fixing commit records the viewport run; the 21 July evidence review independently confirmed the source change and regression-test addition, not a new rerun of that full matrix.

The constraint is narrower than the slogan

Within the standard block-editor controls this theme governs, authors cannot introduce arbitrary colors, gradients, font sizes, or spacing. Those choices resolve to a named token vocabulary in theme.json. Custom code and database-owned content remain governed through review, snapshots, and verification.

A recurring class of palette-review work moves from manual enforcement into the authoring system. That does not make drift impossible everywhere; it makes the governed editor choices explicit, testable, and cheaper to change.
